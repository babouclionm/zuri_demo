How earning would work

A simple path from guest intent to partner revenue.

This is a prototype flow, not a live payment system. It shows how ZURI can become financially useful to partners.

1Guest opens ZURI

They choose a city, category, mood, budget, transport style, or Ask ZURI plan.

2ZURI surfaces partners

Relevant paid partner lanes appear inside experience cards, map links, and concierge plans.

3Guest requests service

Future booking or request logic can capture the lead, handoff, commission, or referral source.

4Partner tracks earnings

A later dashboard can show requests, conversions, revenue, payout status, and city performance.
